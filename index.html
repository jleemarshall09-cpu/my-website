Common Questions

Questions before you apply?

Quick answers about who DogsOnly is for, how the process works, and what to expect.

Who is DogsOnly for? +
College students and entry-level candidates (0–3 years experience) who are struggling to get interviews or land offers. If you're early in your career and feel lost in the job search, this is built for you.
What happens in a coaching session? +
Depending on your plan, we review your resume, sharpen your positioning, and prepare you for the interviews DogsOnly helps generate. You'll leave with a clearer game plan and stronger answers.
How do I prepare for the session? +
Fill out the application form honestly. Have your resume ready and know what role or internship you're targeting. That's it, we handle the rest.
Is this guaranteed to get me a job? +
No one can guarantee an offer, and anyone who says they can is lying to you. What we do guarantee: you'll leave with a sharper resume and stronger interview answers than when you came in.
Do you work with all industries? +
Yes, tech, finance, marketing, sales, consulting, and more. If you're applying for entry-level roles or internships and want to stand out, we can help regardless of industry.
